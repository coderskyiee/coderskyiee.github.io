About the app

Escape is a Fashion Magazine application. Releasing initially for the android, we'd be launching for iOS shortly. As the name suggests Escape is the mini mag which would be releasing new editions weekly for you to slide on. We are here to assist you what to buy, where to shop, where to go and to entertain and update you.

Careers

about us

We at Escape are looking forward to be a living, breathing, epitome of Fashion. Fashion is more than just clothes, shoes, bags, furs and pearls, it is also a tool. It is a beautiful source of expression. It is Art! And we are here to celebrate such artists. Showcasing the works of celebrated designers, the high end haute couture, the History. Taking the History hand in hand with the Future, we would be celebrating the creation, reform and rebuild of the fashion industry. We would also be showcasing people who entertain us and bring us such nuances of emotions through words, music, humour, drama and art. Plunge into the world of Artists in a personalized and highly crafted editions, Escape: so that without much ado we blend in.

To quote Coco Chanel:

The best things in life are free. The second best are very expensive.
